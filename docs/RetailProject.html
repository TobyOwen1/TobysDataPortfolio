Retail Sales Dashboard: Deep Dive

This interactive dashboard was built in Power BI using a cleaned and structured retail dataset. It blends SQL based preprocessing with visual storytelling to surface actionable business insights. Key features include:

• Sales Trends Over Time

Line and column charts display monthly revenue and profit performance, enabling quick identification of peak periods and seasonal patterns.

• Top Performing Products

Dynamic ranking visuals highlight best selling items by quantity and profit, with slicers for category and store location.

• Store Level Comparison

Bar charts compare sales and margins across differen store branches to uncover underperforming regions or growth opportunities.

• Customer Demographics Breakdown

Age groups and gender distributions are visualized to help tailor marketing and inventory decisions to target segments.

• Drill Through & Filtering

Users can drill from high level KPIs into specific product or customer views, with filters allowing exploration across time periods, store names or product categories
