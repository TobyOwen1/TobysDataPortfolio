About me

Hi there! My name's Toby, and my journey with data began a decade ago...

For years I used data to drive decisions as a retail manager, learning firsthand how insights translate into real world outcomes. In 2024, I transitioned into my first official data role an opportunity that allowed me to manage, manipulate, and visualise data professionally.

My experience in retail taught me how to read, analyse, and act on data effectively. Over time, I’ve deepened my technical expertise, building strong skills in Excel and expanding my knowledge into SQL and Power BI. Now, I not only analyse and sort data, I shape it into meaningful insights that drive decision making.

This page is dedicated to the projects I create, refine, and solve using SQL and Power BI. As a Database Analyst for a leading marketing company, I'm honing my skills and preparing for the next chapter in my career.
